Messaging Automation Platform

We are a messaging automation company that provides WhatsApp Business messaging solutions for businesses. We help clients manage, automate, and scale customer communication using the WhatsApp Business Platform and Meta messaging services.

1. Services We Provide

  • WhatsApp Business Account setup and management
  • Automated messaging workflows and chatbots
  • Customer support automation
  • CRM and system integrations via APIs and webhooks
  • Messaging analytics and reporting tools

2. Role in Data Processing

We act strictly as a technical service provider / data processor. We process WhatsApp Business messaging data only on behalf of our clients, who are the data controllers.

3. Meta Platform Compliance

We fully comply with Meta’s developer and platform policies:

  • Meta Platform Terms
  • Meta Privacy Policy Requirements

4. Data We Process

  • Phone numbers of users interacting with WhatsApp Business Accounts
  • Message content sent and received via WhatsApp APIs
  • Message metadata (timestamps, delivery status)
  • Client configuration and integration data

5. How We Use Data

We use data only to:

  • Deliver messaging automation services
  • Route WhatsApp messages between clients and users
  • Provide analytics and reporting
  • Maintain system reliability and performance

We do not sell personal data.

6. Data Sharing

  • Shared with the client (business owner)
  • Shared with Meta (WhatsApp Business API infrastructure)
  • Shared with secure infrastructure providers (hosting, logging, monitoring)

7. Security Measures

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure API authentication
  • Access control and role-based permissions
  • Audit logging and monitoring

8. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and support troubleshooting and audit requirements.

9. Client Responsibilities

  • Obtain valid consent before messaging users
  • Comply with WhatsApp Business Messaging policies
  • Avoid spam, abuse, or unlawful messaging

10. Acceptable Use

Users of our platform must NOT:

  • Send unsolicited spam messages
  • Engage in fraud or impersonation
  • Use the platform for illegal activities
  • Violate Meta policies or WhatsApp rules

11. Service Dependency

Our platform depends on Meta’s WhatsApp Business infrastructure. Availability, messaging limits, and functionality are subject to Meta’s policies and system availability.

12. Limitation of Liability

We are not responsible for:

  • Message delivery failures caused by Meta systems
  • Account restrictions imposed by Meta
  • Misuse of the platform by clients
